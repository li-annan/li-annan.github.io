NUS-PRO: A New Visual Tracking Challenge

NUS-PRO is the abbreviation for 'NUS People and Rigid Objects Dataset', which contains 365 image sequences with manually labeled bounding boxes and occlusion status. It is designed for evaluating algorithms of object tracking.
